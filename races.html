Competitive Runs

Competitive runs are typically divided into categories based on age and gender, with participants competing against others in their respective categories. They may also have different divisions based on experience, such as novice or elite, to ensure fair competition. These runs are often well-organized events that may require registration fees and offer prizes for top finishers. They may also be associated with a charity or a cause and serve as a way to raise awareness or funds for a particular issue or organization.
