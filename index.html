About the Company

RunSC was founded in 2002 by Susan Smith. Susan was born and raised in Greenville SC and was a competitive runners all throughout highschool. She continued her running career at Clemson University and Graduated in 1999. Although this ended her professional running career, her love for running never died. Susan wanted to create community of passionate runners and allow them to connect with one another at any skill level. RunSC offers that connection through running groups, competitive races, and general information about trails and courses located in South Carolina. Our goal is to provide accountability and support through our community and to help keep current and future runners motivated.

Anne Smith, CEO of RunSC
